Qissa 2022

Qissa (The Human Library) is an endeavour to curate and compile a library that brings out the capacity of empathy innate to people - people from within SNU, like you, who have walked across all terrains of life. It is, in the most honest sense of the phrase, an exhibition of life. Each 'human book' is given a spot (usually a canopy) where they narrate their story to a small audience. The listeners are limited to a small number to keep this experience personal.
