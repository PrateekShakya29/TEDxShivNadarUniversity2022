Ashwani Garg
"Was it my Fault?"
How does a six year old deal with abuse she did not even know she was enduring? How does she fend for herself in a family that should have protected her? "You'll have a better story to tell. You can be shaped, dented but not broken." In a compilation of personal experiences, titled, "Was it my Fault?" Teacup talks about her journey towards healing - a journey consisting of ups and downs, highs and lows, and a life graph that is never constant.
Sexual Abuse, Verbal Abuse, Bodyshaming, Bullying, Family Feuds.
